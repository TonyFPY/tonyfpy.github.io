About

Apr 16, 2022
2 minute read

Hi, I am so glad to see you here and thank you for your interests in learning more about me.

Currently, I am a Master’s student @ Brown University (one of the eight private Ivy League research universities) with a concentration in Computer Science. In the summer of 2021, I completed my undergraduate study @ University of Nottingham (top 100 in QS Global Ranking) with a first-class honors in Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence. Also, I had an opportunity to receive an academic training @ University of British Columbia (a top-3 university in Canada) during the summer of 2019.

After accomplishing my undergraduate study, I worked as a research and development engineer intern (Automobile Software) @ NIO, a leading company in the premium smart electric vehicle market, where I devoted myself in designing and exploring digital solutions to create a joyful user-vechle interaction experience. Before that, I used to be a software engineer intern (Video Streaming) @ Juphoon, focusing on the optimization of live video streaming to increase user engagement with high-fidelity video quality while deliver smooth and interruption-free experience.

My main interest lies in the exploration of interactive systems to shape a better user experience. My past projects cover the topics of data-driven modelling and interactive intelligence development. Feel free to contact me if you have some innovative ideas.

I am willing to tell you more about myself and please contact me at my e-mail.
